Danielle Tatarian

Vocalist

Danielle is a vocalist and private music teacher who has been singing and studying music since the age of 10. She has a Bachelors in Music from Cornish College of the Arts in Seattle where she performed in masterclasses for Michael Delos, David McDade of the Seattle Opera, Nancy Zylstra, and Morgan Smith. Danielle appeared as Carrie Mae in Libby Larson’s one act opera, The Silver Fox and made her debut with the Seattle Gilbert and Sullivan Society in their production of Yeomen of the Guard. She is currently writing, composing, and producing her first EP, which will be releases in summer 2018. If you’re interested in private voice or piano lessons (remotely or in person), please contact her below.
